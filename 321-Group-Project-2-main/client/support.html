Support by Role

Common Student Questions:
  • How do I access my lessons? - Log in and click the "Lessons" tab in your dashboard
  • How do I track my progress? - Visit your dashboard to see completed lessons, earned badges, and statistics
  • Can I use AQE offline? - Yes! The app works offline after you've loaded content while online
  • How do I earn badges? - Complete lessons and achieve high scores to unlock achievements
  • What if I forget my password? - Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen or contact your teacher

Student Support Email: students@aqeducation.org

Common Teacher Questions:
  • How do I add students? - Go to "My Students" and click "Add Student" or share access codes
  • How do I create assignments? - Visit "Practice Materials" and click "Create Assignment"
  • Can I see student progress? - Yes! Each student profile shows detailed progress and statistics
  • How do I create custom content? - Use the "Practice Materials" section to create questions and exercises
  • Can I assign Digital Library books? - Yes! Browse the library and click "Assign to Students"

Teacher Support Email: teachers@aqeducation.org

Common Parent Questions:
  • How do I link my child's account? - Go to "My Children" and add your child's email or access code
  • How can I monitor progress? - View your child's dashboard to see lessons completed and scores
  • Can I assign extra lessons? - Yes! You can assign Digital Library books and practice materials
  • Is my child's data safe? - Absolutely. We follow strict privacy regulations. See our Privacy Policy
  • How do I delete my child's account? - Contact us at privacy@aqeducation.org

Parent Support Email: parents@aqeducation.org

Common Admin Questions:
  • How do I manage users? - Visit the "User Management" tab in your admin dashboard
  • How do I add content? - Use "Content Management" to upload lessons, books, and materials
  • How do I configure SMS/USSD? - Visit the "SMS/USSD Accessibility" tab to set up keywords and flows
  • Can I see system analytics? - Yes! The admin dashboard shows platform-wide statistics
  • How do I configure settings? - Visit "System Settings" to manage grade levels, subjects, and features

Admin Support Email: admin@aqeducation.org

Technical Support

Troubleshooting
  • App won't load? Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Offline mode not working? Ensure you've accessed content online first
  • Dark mode issues? Try refreshing the page or clearing cache
  • Translation not working? Select your language from the language dropdown
  • Videos not playing? Check your internet connection and browser settings
System Requirements
  • Browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
  • Internet: Required for initial load; offline access available after
  • Mobile: iOS 12+, Android 8+
  • Screen: Minimum 320px width (mobile-friendly)
  • JavaScript: Must be enabled
Need More Help?

For technical issues, email our tech team at tech@aqeducation.org with:

  • Your browser and device information
  • A description of the problem
  • Screenshots if applicable
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
